## 🚀 What I'm building — APEX

<p align="center">
  <a href="https://apexops.pro/">
    <img src="./assets/apex.png" alt="APEX — Agentic Platform Engineering eXperience for Azure" width="760" />
  </a>
</p>

**[APEX](https://github.com/jonathan-vella/apex)** is an *Agentic Platform Engineering eXperience* for Azure — a multi-agent workflow that turns plain-language infrastructure intent into deployable **Bicep or Terraform**, with human approval gates across the whole lifecycle.

> **AI prepares. Humans decide.** Every requirement, architecture, and plan is drafted and challenged by AI, then approved by a human before anything ships.

<p align="center">
  <a href="https://github.com/jonathan-vella/apex"><img src="https://img.shields.io/github/stars/jonathan-vella/apex?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&label=Star%20APEX&color=0078D4&labelColor=1a1a2e" alt="Star APEX"/></a>
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/E2E_Bicep-88%2F100_·_Grade_B-2ea043?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=1a1a2e" alt="E2E Bicep benchmark"/>
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Terraform-dual--track-844FBA?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=1a1a2e" alt="Terraform dual track"/>
</p>
